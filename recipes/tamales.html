Homemade Tamales



Description

  • Prep Time:35 minutes
  • Cook Time:3 hours
  • Total Time:3 hours 35 minutes
  • Servings:16

This is the recipe for some homemade tamales for anyone that has enjoyed these for years.
The pork can be substituted for chicken or beef. These are good served with refried beans and salad.


Ingredients


Filling:

  • 1 1/4 pounds pork loin
  • 1 large onion, halved
  • 1 garlic clove

Chile Sauce:

  • 4 dried chile peppers
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

Husks and Dough:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package dried corn husks
  • 2/3 cups lard
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can of beef broth, or as needed
  • 2 cups masa harina
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sour cream
